    Frequently asked questions

    When should I start seeds indoors?

    Most annuals: 6‑8 weeks before last frost. Check your zone and the seed packet for specifics.

    How often should I water raised beds?

    Deeply and less often: 1‑2 times per week, depending on weather. Use the finger test — dry 2 inches down means water.

    What is the best mulch for vegetable gardens?

    Straw, shredded leaves, or grass clippings (dry). They suppress weeds, retain moisture, and feed the soil.
