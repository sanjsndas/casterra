Cookies Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences. At Casterra · Briarpath Retreat, we believe in clarity — just like a well-tended path.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our site, and improve your experience. We use only minimal, respectful cookies.

Types of cookies we use

CookiePurposeDuration

Our commitment: We do not use tracking cookies for advertising, and we never share cookie data with third parties. Your visit stays as private as a hidden glade.

How you can manage cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies (though some site features may not work as expected).
  • Delete existing cookies.
  • Set preferences for specific sites.

To adjust your settings, check your browser’s help section. For more information, visit aboutcookies.org.
